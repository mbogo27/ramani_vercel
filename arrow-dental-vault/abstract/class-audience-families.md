# Abstract Class — Audience: Nairobi Families

## Who This Is

Parents and guardians in Nairobi metropolitan area seeking dental care for their children. Decision-maker is the parent — the patient is the child.

## Core Concerns

- Is it safe and gentle for my child?
- Will my child be scared?
- Is it affordable for the whole family?
- Do they actually have experience with kids?

## Primary Booking Triggers

- "Pediatric care available now"
- "Accepts SHA/NHIF & Other Insurance covers"
- "No referral needed"

## Tone Calibration

Speak to the parent's anxiety, not the child's.
Warm, reassuring, educational.
Lead with the fear-free framing before any clinical detail.

## Key Friction Removers

- Emphasize ages 2–16 explicitly — parents want to know their child's age is covered
- "We make dental visits something kids look forward to" — use this line for high-anxiety parent signals
