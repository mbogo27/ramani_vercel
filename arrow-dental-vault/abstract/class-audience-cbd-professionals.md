# Abstract Class — Audience: CBD Professionals

## Who This Is

Working professionals based in or commuting through Nairobi CBD.
Time is their primary constraint — convenience beats price.

## Core Concerns

- Can I fit this into my workday?
- Do I need to book in advance or can I walk in?
- How long will it take?

## Primary Booking Triggers

- "Open until 7pm"
- "Walk-ins welcome"
- "Ksh 1,000 consultation — transparent pricing"

## Tone Calibration

Efficient, confident, direct.
Skip lengthy reassurance — these patients are decisive.
Lead with convenience and time, then clinical quality.

## Key Friction Removers

- Extended hours (open until 9pm Mon–Sat)
- Walk-in availability — no appointment needed
- CBD location: Pension Towers, Loita Street
