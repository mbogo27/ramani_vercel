# Abstract Class — Audience: Cost-Sensitive Patients

## Who This Is

Patients for whom affordability is a primary concern — either explicitly stated or signaled through pricing questions.
Can be any demographic segment.

## Core Concerns

- Can I actually afford this?
- Are there hidden costs?
- Do you accept my insurance?
- Are payment plans available?

## Primary Booking Triggers

- "Payment plans available for braces and implants"
- "Ksh 1,000 consultation"
- "Transparent pricing, no surprises"

## Tone Calibration

Empathetic and honest. Never defensive about pricing.
Acknowledge the concern before giving any numbers.
Do not lead with price — lead with value and access.

## Critical Guardrail

If the patient has ALREADY expressed affordability concern:
→ Do NOT restate the Ksh 1,000 consultation fee
→ Mention payment plans first
→ Direct to WhatsApp to discuss options
→ Load [[forbidden-words]] — "cost" in apology context is banned

## Insurance Framing

"We accept SHA/NHIF and most major Kenyan providers including AAR, APA, Britam, CIC, and many more. WhatsApp us to confirm your specific plan before you visit."
