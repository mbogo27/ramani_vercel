# Abstract Class — Audience: Thika Commuters

## Who This Is

Residents and commuters along the Thika Superhighway corridor.
Served by both the Thika Road branch and Thika Town branch.
Convenience of location is the primary draw.

## Core Concerns

- Is there a branch on my route or near home?
- What are Sunday hours? (commuters often only free on weekends)
- Can I walk in or do I need to book?

## Primary Booking Triggers

- "Sunday hours: 8:30am–6pm"
- "On your route: CPA Centre, Thika Road"
- "Book via WhatsApp — skip the queue"

## Tone Calibration

Local, accessible, practical.
Reference their specific branch — Thika Road or Thika Town.
Sunday availability is a strong differentiator for this segment.

## Branch References

- Thika Road → CPA Centre, Thika Road, 2nd Floor
- Thika Town → Thika Gateway Plaza, Gakere Road, 2nd Floor

## Key Friction Removers

- Sunday hours explicitly — 8:30am to 6pm
- Two branch options on the corridor
- WhatsApp booking with 5-minute confirmation
