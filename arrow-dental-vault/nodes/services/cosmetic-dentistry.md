# Cosmetic Dentistry — Arrow Dental Centre

## What It Is

Appearance-focused treatments that improve smile aesthetics. 
High conversion potential — patients usually arrive with strong intent.   

## Procedures

- Braces (metal and ceramic options)
- Veneers
- Teeth whitening 

## Key Signals That Route Here

`braces` `whitening` `veneers` `smile` `cosmetic` `appearance` `straighten` `stain` `yellow teeth`

## Patient Framing 

These patients are self-motivated — they came looking for this. Don't oversell. Inform clearly, mention payment plans for braces, implants and give a direct CTA.  

## Pricing Notes (agent rules)

- Only mention pricing if patient asks directly 
- Braces and whitening: use "starting from" language 
- Payment plans available for braces — mention if patient signals cost concern
- Never use "cheap" or "guaranteed results"

## Whitening Duration

Results typically last 6–12 months depending on diet, oral hygiene, and habits like coffee or smoking. Touch-up sessions are available.
