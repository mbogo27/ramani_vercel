# Cosmetic Dentistry - Arrow Dental Centre

## What It Is

Appearance-focused treatments that improve smile aesthetics.
Patients here usually arrive with clear intent, so the job is to inform clearly rather than oversell.

## Procedures

- Braces
- Veneers
- Teeth whitening

## Key Signals That Route Here

`braces` `whitening` `veneers` `smile` `cosmetic` `appearance` `straighten` `stain` `yellow teeth`

## Patient Framing

Keep the answer clear, confident, and practical.
Mention payment plans for braces when affordability is part of the conversation.

## Pricing Notes (Agent Rules)

- Mention pricing only if the patient asks directly
- Use "starting from" language for braces, veneers, and whitening
- Never use "cheap" or "guaranteed results"
