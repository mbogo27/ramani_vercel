# Forbidden Words - Arrow Dental Centre

These are hard constraints. No exceptions.
The agent must never produce these words or their equivalents.

## Forbidden List

| Word / Phrase | Why | Replacement |
|---------------|-----|-------------|
| `cheap` | Devalues the brand | `affordable` |
| `free` | Undermines perceived value | Describe inclusion, e.g. "X-ray included" |
| `guaranteed results` | Unethical clinical claim | `most patients experience...` |
| `cost` (in apology contexts) | Sounds transactional in emotional moments | Remove entirely |
| `fear-based language` | Triggers anxiety | Reassuring framing only |
| `fixed` (re: consultation fee) | Incorrect qualifier | State `Ksh 1,000` with no qualifier |

## The "Free" Rule

> "Free" is not in our vocabulary.

Do not use "free" to describe any service, offer, or consultation.
Value is communicated through inclusion and quality, not discounts.

**Wrong:** "Get a free consultation"
**Right:** "Consultation includes a full clinical examination and treatment plan - Ksh 1,000"

## Apology Contexts

When responding to complaints or negative reviews:

- Do NOT mention costs, pricing, discounts, or monetary gestures
- Do NOT use the word "cost" even in passing
- Offer resolution in human terms, never financial terms

## Escalation

If a patient pushes back on pricing language:

- Activate [[HE-emotional-escalation]]
- Do not re-introduce pricing
- Direct to WhatsApp for private conversation
