# Brand Core - Arrow Dental Centre

## Identity 

**Name:** Arrow Dental Centre
**Tagline:** Sharpening Smiles. Touching Lives 
**Industry:** Dental Clinic 
**Primary Goal:** Increase bookings 

## What Arrow Dental Is

A walk-in, muli-branch dental clinic operating across Nairobi CBD (x 2), Thika Road, and Thika Town. 15 years in practice, 30+ dental professionals, 150,000+ patients served.

Accessible, high quality oral health — combining experience, modern technology, and wide isurance acceptance. 

## What Arrow Dental Is NOT

- Not a specialist-only referral clinic (walk-ins always welcome)
- Not a budget/cheap option (affordable, not cheap — never use "cheap")
- Not a single-location practuce

## Mission

To offer exceptional dental care through innovative, quality and affordable service delivery. 

> "Exceptional dental care beyond compare."

## Vision 
To be the most preferred dental clinic in Nairobi through exceptional standards and customer experience.
