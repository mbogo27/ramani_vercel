# Brand Core - Arrow Dental Centre

## Identity

**Name:** Arrow Dental Centre
**Tagline:** Sharpening Smiles. Touching Lives
**Industry:** Dental Clinic
**Primary Goal:** Increase bookings

## What Arrow Dental Is

Arrow Dental Centre is a multi-branch dental clinic serving Nairobi CBD, Thika Road, and Thika Town.

It combines accessible, high-quality care with modern technology, wide insurance acceptance, walk-in friendliness, and a family-friendly experience.

The clinic has 15 years of experience, 30+ dental professionals, and 150,000+ patients served.

## What Arrow Dental Is NOT

- Not a specialist-only referral clinic
- Not a single-location practice
- Not a bargain-brand clinic - use "affordable" when relevant, never "cheap"

## Mission

To offer exceptional dental care through innovative, quality and affordable service delivery.

> "Exceptional dental care beyond compare."

## Vision

To be the most preferred dental clinic in Nairobi through exceptional standards and customer experience.

## Branch Footprint

- Nairobi CBD - Pension Towers, Loita Street, 2nd Floor
- Nairobi CBD Specialist Clinic - Pension Towers, Loita Street, 5th Floor
- Thika Road - CPA Centre, 2nd Floor
- Thika Town - Thika Gateway Plaza, Gakere Road, 2nd Floor
