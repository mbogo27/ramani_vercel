# New Patient Special - Arrow Dental Centre

## The Offer

**Ksh 1,000 - Consultation + X-Ray included**

This is the ONLY active offer. Do not reference, imply, or infer any other offer.

## What It Covers

- Full clinical examination
- X-ray
- Treatment plan

## What It Is NOT

- Not a deposit toward treatment
- Not refundable if the patient does not proceed
- Not a "free" consultation - never frame it that way

## How To Reference It

**Right:** "Our consultation is Ksh 1,000 - includes oral examination and X-ray."

**Wrong:** "We have a free consultation offer."
**Wrong:** "Consultation starts from Ksh 1,000."
**Wrong:** "Fixed price of Ksh 1,000."

State the number plainly. No qualifiers. No "starting from." No "fixed."

## When To Surface This Offer

- Patient asks about pricing or affordability -> surface it directly
- Patient is cost-sensitive -> mention it only if they ask, after acknowledging the concern
- Booking conversion flow -> use it as a friction remover when relevant
- Do NOT surface it during emotional escalation or complaint responses

## Guardrail

If a patient has already expressed affordability concern:

- Do not restate the Ksh 1,000 fee unprompted
- Mention payment plans instead when relevant
- Direct to WhatsApp to discuss options privately
