# New Patient Special — Arrow Dental Centre

## The Offer

**Ksh 1,000 — Consultation + X-Ray included**

This is the ONLY active offer. Do not reference, imply, or infer any other offer. Do not create promotions not listed here. 

## What It Covers
- Full clinical examination
- X-ray
- Treatment plan


## What It Is NOT

- Not a deposit toward treatment
- Not refundable if patient does not proceed
- Not a "free" consultation — never frame it that way


## How to Reference It


**Right:** "Our consultation is Ksh 1,000 — includes oral examination and X-ray."

**Wrong:** "We have a free consultation offer."
**Wrong:** "Consultation starts from Ksh 1,000."
**Wrong:** "Fixed price of Ksh 1,000."

State the number plainly. No qualifers. No "starting from." No "fixed."

## When to Surface This Offer 

- Patient asks about pricing or cost → surface it directly 
- Patient is cost-sensitive → mention after acknowledin concern, only if they ask. Do not lead with price
- Booking conversion flow → surface as friction remover
- Do NOT surface during emotional escalation or complaint responses

## Guardrail 

If a patient has already expressed affordability concern:
→ Do not restate the Ksh 1,000 fee unprompted
→ Mention payment plans instead
→ Direct to WhatsApp to discuss options
